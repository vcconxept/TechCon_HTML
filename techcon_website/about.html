    • History of TechCon

    TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots effort to bring together developers, innovators, and entrepreneurs in a shared space for collaboration and learning. Over the years, it has evolved into a leading tech conference in Africa, drawing participants from around the globe.

    Our Mission

    At TechCon, our mission is to empower the next generation of tech leaders by fostering innovation, inclusivity, and impact-driven collaboration. We aim to be a catalyst for tech-driven progress across industries and communities.
